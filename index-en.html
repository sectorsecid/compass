How to allow compass sensor access
On iPhone (Safari):
  1. Tap "Start / Calibrate Sensors" and allow the "Motion & Orientation Access" pop-up when it appears.
  2. If no pop-up appears, go to Settings → Safari → Motion & Orientation Access and turn it on.
On Android (Chrome):
  1. Allow the location permission prompt — the orientation sensor usually activates automatically.
  2. Wave your phone in a figure-8 motion a few times to recalibrate the magnetometer if the heading looks off.
  3. Keep the phone away from metal objects, magnets, or speaker cases that can interfere with the sensor.
